Age of Aquarius is a series of seven self-portraits, each visually reinterpreting a tarot card from my most recent reading. This reading, known as a chakra spread, pulls seven cards for each one of the seven energy centers along my spinal cord. Starting from the tailbone and ending at the pineal gland: Root, Sacral, Solar Plexus, Heart, Throat, Third Eye, and Crown. These images serve as a reminder of where I was in life, mentally, physically, emotionally, and spiritually, the night that Pluto shifted into the constellation of Aquarius.
